Submit your entry and be part of a global youth movement.

We invite young changemakers to express bold ideas and creative solutions through powerful essays. Please read the guidelines below before submitting.

Submission Guidelines

  • Youth leadership in conflict zones and peacebuilding
  • The role of youth in climate action and environmental justice
  • Access to education and digital equity

Theme

"The Power of Youth: Leading Change from the Ground Up"

Eligibility

Youth under the age of 25 from any country

Age Categories

  • Under 13
  • 13–17
  • 18–24
  • 25+ (Open Youth Category)

Essay Requirements

  • Word Limit: 1000–1500 words
  • Format: PDF or DOCX only
  • File Name Format: YourFirstName_YourLastName_EssayTitle.pdf
  • Only one entry per participant
  • Essays must be original and unpublished

Recognition

  • Winners will be featured on our Instagram page and website
  • Digital certificates for all valid entries
  • Top entries may receive volunteer opportunities with Ayoniz MUN

Terms & Conditions

  1. The essay must be your own original work. Plagiarized or AI-generated content will be disqualified.
  2. By submitting, you give us the right to publish your name, country, essay title, and an excerpt on our website and Instagram.
  3. Only one entry is allowed per participant.
  4. We reserve the right to verify age and identity if needed.
  5. All essays must follow the given format and word count rules.
  6. Judging is anonymous and final.
  7. Your data will only be used for competition communication.
  8. Selected winners may receive volunteer opportunities with Ayoniz MUN.
