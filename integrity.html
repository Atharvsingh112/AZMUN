Our Commitment to Integrity

At Ayoniz MUN, integrity is at the heart of everything we do. As a youth-led organization, we believe in upholding the highest ethical standards to foster a community that thrives on honesty, responsibility, and respect.

What Integrity Means to Us

  • Academic Honesty: All submissions and contributions to our programs must be original. We strictly prohibit plagiarism, AI-generated content without disclosure, and any form of misrepresentation.
  • Fairness and Transparency: Our evaluation processes are impartial, with clear guidelines and anonymous judging to ensure a level playing field for all participants.
  • Respectful Conduct: We expect all delegates, volunteers, and team members to interact with one another in a respectful and professional manner—online and offline.
  • Confidentiality: Any personal data or submitted content is handled responsibly and only for official purposes related to Ayoniz MUN.
  • Leadership by Example: We train and expect our leadership team and volunteers to be role models of ethical conduct, inclusive leadership, and constructive dialogue.
